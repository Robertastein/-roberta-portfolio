Roberta Stein is an Italian visual artist working with moving images, sound, and photography.

Her practice is grounded in personal and collective experience, exploring how social trauma shapes bodies, identities, and relationships.

Through raw, intimate collaborations with people close to her, she builds sensory narratives that blur the line between the individual and the collective.
